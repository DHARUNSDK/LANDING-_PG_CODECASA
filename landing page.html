Welcome to the Equine Heritage of India!

Discover a variety of majestic horses native to India, each with its own unique characteristics and charm.

MARWARI HORSE

Known for distinctive inward-curving ears and a proud carriage.

Specifications: Height: 14 to 16 hands, Weight: 900 to 1200 pounds

Location: Commonly found in the Marwar region of Rajasthan.

KATHIAWARI HORSE

Recognized for a fine head, long neck, and a slightly dished profile.

Specifications: Height: 14 to 15.2 hands, Weight: 900 to 1200 pounds

Location: Native to the Kathiawar Peninsula in Gujarat.

ZANSKARI HORSE

Adapted to harsh mountainous terrain, known for endurance and strength.

Specifications: Height: 12 to 14 hands, Weight: 700 to 900 pounds

Location: Indigenous to the Zanskar Valley in Ladakh.

MALANI HORSE

Descendant of the Kathiawari breed, known for endurance and versatility.

Specifications: Height: 13.2 to 15.2 hands, Weight: 800 to 1000 pounds

Location: Found in the Malani region of Rajasthan.
